Quaid e Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

1876 - 1948

Founder of Pakistan

Muhammad Ali Jinnah was the founder of Pakistan. He was a barrister, politician, and the leader of the All-India Muslim League from 1913 until the inception of Pakistan on 14 August 1947, and then as the Dominion of Pakistan's first governor-general until his death. He is revered in Pakistan as the Quaid-e-Azam ("Great Leader") and Baba-e-Qaum ("Father of the Nation"). His birthday is observed as a national holiday in Pakistan.
